## About Edinburgh Photographic Society

Edinburgh Photographic Society (EPS) has been active for more than 150 years and, currently, has around 200 members. There are several meetings per week during the season, which runs from September to April, with informal meetings organised during the summer. Our [What’s On](https://www.edinburghphotographicsociety.co.uk/wp/whats-on-v2/) page has details of forthcoming meetings and our season’s syllabus.

Our International Exhibition is held as part of the Edinburgh Fringe festival and attracts entries from all over the world.&nbsp;

Edinburgh Photographic Society is a Scottish Charitable Incorporated Organisation. You can find our constitution and operational obligations on the our [Governance page](https://edinburghphotographicsociety.co.uk/wp/people-and-governance-v2). You can see images of EPS Council members and other volunteers on our [people page.](https://www.edinburghphotographicsociety.co.uk/wp/people-v2/)
